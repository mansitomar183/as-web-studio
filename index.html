FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

How long does it take to build a website? +
Typically 5–7 working days for a Starter or Business website. Premium or custom websites can take 10–15 days depending on complexity. We always keep you updated at every stage of the process.
Do I need technical knowledge to manage my website? +
Not at all! We build your website and hand it over with a simple guide. For updates or changes, you can simply WhatsApp us and we'll take care of it. Many of our clients have zero technical background.
What is included in the website price — domain and hosting? +
Our packages cover design and development. Domain (approx. ₹600–900/year) and hosting (approx. ₹2,000–5,000/year depending on plan) are separate. We'll guide you on which hosting suits your budget and needs — we can handle the entire setup for you.
Will my website rank on Google? +
Yes! Every website we build is optimized for SEO from day one — fast loading, mobile-friendly, proper meta tags, schema markup, and Google Maps integration. For stronger results, we recommend our Premium Website or Social Media plans which include deeper SEO work.
Can I see design samples before paying? +
Absolutely. You can view our full portfolio and we'll share similar website examples from our past work before you commit. We also offer a free consultation call to understand your vision before starting.
How does social media management work? +
We handle everything — content creation, graphic design, caption writing, hashtag research, scheduling, and posting. You'll receive a monthly content calendar for approval before we publish. No need to lift a finger!
Do you offer EMI or installment payment? +
Yes! We offer a simple 50/50 payment plan — 50% advance to begin work and 50% on delivery. For larger projects, custom payment schedules can be arranged. Contact us on WhatsApp to discuss.
What types of businesses do you work with? +
We work with all types of local and small businesses — restaurants, dhabas, cafes, salons, gyms, clothing shops, medical clinics, coaching institutes, hotels, startups, and more. If you have a business, we can help you grow it online.
